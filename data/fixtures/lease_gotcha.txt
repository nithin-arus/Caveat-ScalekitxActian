Residential Lease Agreement

1. Premises
Landlord leases to Tenant the residential unit located at 500 Valencia Street, Unit 9, San Francisco, CA.

2. Rent
Tenant will pay monthly rent of $2,950 on the first day of each month during the lease term.

3. Nonrefundable Deposit
The security deposit is nonrefundable and may be applied by Landlord in Landlord's sole discretion. Landlord may retain deposit amounts for general wear and tear, odor, paint touch-up, carpet cleaning, or administrative review.

4. Construction and Quiet Enjoyment
Tenant waives claims and rent reduction rights for construction, noise, dust, renovation, interruption, or loss of access caused by Landlord or neighboring work.

5. Relocation
Landlord may relocate Tenant to another unit at any time for any reason upon 5 days' notice.

6. Essential Services
Landlord is not responsible for interruption of water, heat, gas, electricity, internet, elevator, trash, or other services for any reason.

7. Move-Out Notice
Tenant must provide 90 days' written move-out notice or pay two additional months of rent after vacating.

8. Attorney Fees
Tenant shall pay Landlord's attorney fees and costs in any dispute, regardless of outcome.

9. Rules
Tenant agrees to comply with all rules now existing and any future rules Landlord adopts, modifies, or revokes at any time.

10. Mold and Pests
Tenant acknowledges the premises may contain mold and releases Landlord from all mold-related claims. Tenant is responsible for all bedbug inspection, treatment, relocation, laundry, disposal, and extermination costs.

11. Appliances
Appliances are provided for Tenant convenience only and Landlord has no duty to repair or replace them.

12. Notices
All notices to Tenant may be sent by email, text, portal, or posting and are deemed received immediately upon sending.
