Residential Lease Agreement

1. Parties and Premises
Landlord leases to Tenant the residential unit located at 123 Maple Street, Unit 4B, San Francisco, CA, for residential use only.

2. Term
The lease begins on July 1, 2026 and ends on June 30, 2027. Renewal requires written agreement by both parties.

3. Rent
Tenant will pay monthly rent of $2,800 on the first day of each month during the lease term.

4. Security Deposit
Tenant will pay a refundable security deposit of $2,800. Deductions are limited to unpaid rent, damage beyond ordinary wear and tear, and other lawful charges. Landlord will provide an itemized statement and return any remaining deposit within the deadline required by applicable law.

5. Late Fees
If rent is not received within 5 days after the due date, Tenant will pay a late fee of $50 or the maximum allowed by law, whichever is less.

6. Repairs and Maintenance
Tenant must promptly notify Landlord of needed repairs. Landlord will maintain the premises, building systems, included appliances, and habitability conditions as required by law. Tenant is responsible for damage caused by Tenant or guests.

7. Entry
Landlord may enter the premises with at least 24 hours' notice during reasonable hours for inspections, repairs, or showings, except in emergencies or as otherwise allowed by law.

8. Utilities
Tenant is responsible for electricity and internet. Landlord is responsible for water, trash, and common-area utilities.

9. Guests and Occupancy
Tenant may have ordinary guests. Guests staying more than 14 consecutive days require Landlord approval, not to be unreasonably withheld.

10. Assignment and Subletting
Tenant may request assignment or sublet approval. Landlord consent may not be unreasonably withheld, conditioned, or delayed.

11. Move-Out
Tenant will return the premises in substantially the same condition as received, ordinary wear and tear excepted.

12. Entire Agreement
This lease and written addenda are the full agreement between the parties.
